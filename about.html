About Editors Gallery

Editors Gallery is a modern freelance platform built to connect content creators and video editors in one seamless ecosystem. From short-form reels to cinematic films, creators can post projects, set budgets, and instantly get matched with skilled editors who fit their style and price range — from as low as ₹20 to as high as ₹10,000 per video.

Our mission is to make collaboration effortless. Editors can showcase their portfolios, apply for projects that match their expertise, and grow their freelance careers — all within one secure and user-friendly platform. With built-in real-time chat, safe payment processing, and premium memberships, Editors Gallery ensures transparency, fair pricing, and creative freedom for all.

Our Vision

Founded by Heyy_letscode. Editors Gallery was created to empower India’s creator community by making collaboration smooth, trusted, and rewarding. We believe in building a world where creators and editors can connect, collaborate, and create magic together — with fairness, opportunity, and creativity at the heart of every project.
